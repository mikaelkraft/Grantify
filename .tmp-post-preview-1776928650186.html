Bisi's story is a testament to the power of vision and determination. When she inherited her father's barren farmland in Oyo State, many people advised her to sell it and move to the city. With the support of the Central Bank of Nigeria's (CBN) Anchor Borrowers' Programme, she was able to access funding and technical expertise to turn her farm into a thriving business.
