Technology Sector

The technology sector in the country is rapidly growing, with many startups and innovation hubs emerging. To access these funds, startups need to have a solid business plan, a unique value proposition, and a clear understanding of their target market. The agriculture and healthcare sectors are also critical areas of focus for funding opportunities in the country.
