Agriculture: A Sector in Need of Innovation the country's agricultural sector is in need of innovation, with many farmers still using traditional methods that are no longer effective. The use of grants to fund sustainable farming practices can help to increase crop yields, improve food security, and provide economic opportunities for rural communities.
